Powerflow enables energy exchange in a reliable and secure manner. It is a control mechanism for devices to communicate and trade energy in a decentralized micro grid.

Overview

Each instance of powerflow contains the following services:
  • edgex services for a powerflow instance
  • trader service to trade and conclude a tx
  • blockchain service to maintain chain
